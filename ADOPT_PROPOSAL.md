# AI-Assisted Crop Development Monitoring
[AI-Assisted Crop Development Monitoring

The Problem

Farmers make important management decisions throughout the season, including fertiliser applications, irrigation, crop protection, and variety selection.

However, there is often no simple way to continuously measure whether those decisions are resulting in the expected crop development throughout the season.

Most existing tools provide snapshots of crop condition rather than tracking how a crop develops over time.

Our Solution

We are developing an AI-assisted crop development monitoring system that combines:

Sentinel satellite observations

Historical field performance

Weather data

AI-driven interpretation

The system learns how each field normally develops and compares the current season against its historical development trajectory.

Rather than asking:

"What does the field look like today?"

we ask:

"Is this crop developing as expected?"

What the System Does

✔ Builds a multi-year historical baseline for each field

✔ Tracks current-season crop development

✔ Detects abnormal development patterns

✔ Identifies fields that are ahead, on track, or behind expectations

✔ Generates field-level alerts and management insights

Example Insight

Field A:

Development Performance: 92/100

Above historical baseline

Consistent development throughout season

Field B:

Development Performance: 88/100

Persistent negative deviation

Early warning generated for field inspection

Innovation Hypothesis

Crop development leaves measurable signatures in satellite observations.

By combining historical trajectories, real-time monitoring, and AI interpretation, we believe abnormal crop development can be identified earlier and more consistently than conventional field observation alone.

ADOPT Trial Objective

To validate whether satellite-derived crop development trajectories correspond to real field conditions and whether AI-generated alerts provide practical value to farmers.

Success will be measured through:

Agreement with observed field conditions

Detection of development deviations

Farmer feedback and usability

Ability to identify issues earlier than standard practice

Long-Term Vision

An AI agronomy assistant that continuously monitors crop development, explains abnormal growth patterns, and provides early-warning insights to support better farm management decisions.
